Eight Years of Moves, One Promise Kept.

From a single van in Al Ghubra to a full fleet covering every governorate — here's the story behind AS House Shifting.

How We Started

A Local Team That Treats Your Move Like Our Own

AS House Shifting started in Al Ghubra with one truck, one crew, and a simple idea — that moving day shouldn't be stressful. Eight years on, word of mouth from families and businesses across Muscat has grown that into a full fleet serving every part of Oman, from Musandam in the north to Salalah in the south.

We still run the business the way we started it: our own trucks, our own trained crews, and a phone that's answered 24 hours a day. No subcontractors handling your belongings, no disappearing after the quote.

  • Trained, uniformed crews — not day labor
  • Our own fleet, sized for villas, apartments & offices
  • Insurance-backed handling on every job
  • Arabic & English speaking coordinators
